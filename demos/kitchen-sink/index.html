About This Demo

This proof-of-concept demonstrates embedding MapX geospatial datasets via the SDK, showcasing disaster risk reduction data from the Eco-DRR Geospatial Datasets project.

Hazard Layers

Explore flood, landslide, cyclone, and tsunami exposure data from global risk models covering return periods and annual physical exposure.

Nature-Based Solutions

See where forest protection and mangrove restoration can reduce flood and storm surge risk — the core of the Eco-DRR approach.

Live Monitoring

Real-time earthquake data (M5.5+) from USGS and active fire tracking at administrative level, updated continuously.
